Privacy Policy

- Updated May 2022


This following document sets forth the Privacy Policy for the CompMates mobile app, as found on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. CompMates is bound by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), which sets out a number of principles concerning the privacy of individuals.


Collection of personal information

All aspects of the CompMates mobile app can be viewed without providing personal information. Personal information such as names can be entered into the app, however, no personal information entered by the user is collected by the app.
CompMates uses the AdMob service to serve ads. Google may use the information that it obtains via the third party cookies, web beacons, and other technologies used in connection with AdMob in accordance with its advertising Privacy Policies. You can visit these third party websites to opt out of cookies for interest based advertising.


Sharing of personal information

CompMates does not collect personally identifiable information. There is no personal information that can be shared to third party companies.


Use of personal information

CompMates does not collect personally identifiable information. CompMates does use advertising data for:
- Third-party advertising to display third-party ads in the app and sharing advertising data with entities who display third-party ads.
- App functionality to enable features.
- Analytics, evaluating user behaviour, understanding the effectiveness of existing product features, planning new features and measuring audience size.


Changes to this Privacy Policy

CompMates reserves the right to make amendments to this Privacy Policy at any time. If you have objections to the Privacy Policy, you should not access or use the site.


Accessing your Personal information

CompMates does not collect personally identifiable information. There is no personal information that can be accessed.


Contacting us
